Repair Services


We are able to provide you with all parts necessary to complete any needed repairs. When we quote a job, we list individual parts needed along with their prices. We offer our customers "factory-direct pricing". When you purchase the parts from us, they still come direct to you from the manufacturer.

Due to differing electrical codes from city to city and state to state, we have found it more cost effective for customers to use a local electrician. If electrical repairs are needed while we are working on a manlift, we are able to assist the electrician, if needed.


"The labor portion of a job can be quoted several ways, including sending a crew of our skilled mechanics to perform the repairs, or just having one our working supervisors on your job site to work with your crew."
