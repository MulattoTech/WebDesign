PermaTron Elevator, Inc. is a full service manlift company that began doing business in 1979 as Perma Associates and Sons. For twenty-six years, we have built our business around the needs of industries that utilize belt manlifts and/or cage elevators (Special Purpose Personnel Elevators).

PermaTron Mission Statement - Our mission is to insure our customer's manlift needs are met by providing the highest quality inspection, repair, and installation service. We believe honesty, integrity and good communication, combined with experienced, safety-minded personnel, allow us to best serve the needs of all industries that utilize manlifts in the daily operation of their business.

"Your most valuable resource is riding on your manlift"

We offer new installation with "factory-direct pricing" on Humphrey Belt Manlifts and Sidney Cage Type Elevators. Other elevator applications that we offer are manual rope and rack and pinion units. We also provide training, repair, inspection and load testing. Our inspections and load tests are governed by ASME A90.1 and A17.1.

We are available to take your calls 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. (Central Time) Monday through Friday; or you can e-mail us, fax us, or leave a voice mail, 24 hours a day.
